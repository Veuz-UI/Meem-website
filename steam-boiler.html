Steam Boilers Rentals

  • Our advanced LOOS steam boilers are engineered to meet the stringent demands of the oil and gas sector.
  • Our boilers are LOOS/BOSCH brand (Germany), which are the most expensive, highest safety chain. They are robust, efficient, and designed to enhance operational performance, providing a continuous and reliable supply of steam for various applications.
  • Our boilers are equipped with Weishaupt burners and Grundfos pumps which are characterized by their outstanding operational reliability, maximum Efficiency and infallible quality.
  • All our operators are Engineers & Technicians that are boiler service specialists
  • Our partner (CATEC) have been the exclusive service agent of LOOS/BOSCH (boiler manufacturer) for the past 35+ years in several MEA region countries, so our operators are technically proficient in trouble-shooting and have the expertise to solve most boiler breakdown problems on-site to avoid any disturbances with rig operations as they provide service to customers on a daily basis
  • All boiler operators also speak Arabic, which makes it easier to communicate with Arab customers
